Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our pricing and plans

What counts as one asset?

One asset is a single 3D model created from your photos. Whether you upload 10 or 20 photos, it counts as one asset. Multiple variations (colors, sizes) of the same product count as separate assets.

Do you offer refunds?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for Growth plan subscriptions. Self-serve assets are processed immediately, but we'll work with you if you're not satisfied with the quality.

Can I upgrade or downgrade anytime?

Yes! You can upgrade to a higher plan at any time. Downgrades take effect at your next billing cycle. Enterprise customers can discuss custom terms with our sales team.

What's included in Enterprise?

Enterprise includes unlimited models, custom branding, on-premise deployment options, dedicated support, and custom integrations. Pricing varies based on your specific requirements.
